Youth Education Grant

For ages 18-25 Funding: $5,000 per academic year Applications open until August 31, 2024

About This Program

The Youth Education Grant provides financial assistance to young adults pursuing post-secondary education. This program is designed to help cover the costs of tuition, books, and living expenses for eligible students.

Funding Details

  • Up to $5,000 per academic year
  • Renewable for up to 4 years
  • Can be used for tuition, books, and living expenses

Eligibility Requirements

  • Must be between 18-25 years old
  • Enrolled in an accredited post-secondary institution
  • Family income below $60,000 per year
  • Maintain a minimum 2.5 GPA

How to Apply

  1. Complete the online application form
  2. Submit proof of enrollment
  3. Provide income verification documents
  4. Include a personal statement (500 words max)
